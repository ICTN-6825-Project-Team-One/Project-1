Best Bang for the Buck
Las Vegas, Nevada

From its days as a sleepy stop along the Union Pacific Railroad, to its modern day evolution as a gambling and entertainment mecca, Las Vegas represents a unique American experience. Travelers will find plenty of low-cost flights, tons of hotel options, an abundant inventory of car rental vendors, and plenty of meal-inclusive lodging packages.
If you can tear yourself away from the craps table (remember: the house always wins), you will be rewarded with a never-ending sea of entertainment options, from Cirque du Soleil to Rod Stewart’s upcoming residency at Caesars Palace. If sightseeing is more your thing, take advantage of Las Vegas’ extensive road system to visit the Hoover Dam, Red Rocks Canyon, Mount Charleston, or Death Valley (just a short drive off I-10). You can also let someone else do the driving and book one-day excursions to the Grand Canyon. Of course, don’t forget to take a tour (on foot or in a trolley) of the famous Las Vegas strip. Cap off the experience by enjoying the vistas from the Eiffel Tower viewing deck (part of Paris Las Vegas), or the Stratosphere Tower (part of the STRAT Hotel and Casino). Few other places in America can top Las Vegas’ combination of variety and affordable transportation/lodging.
